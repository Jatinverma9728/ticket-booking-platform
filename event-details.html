Summer Music Festival

Central Park, New York

June 15, 2023

7:00 PM - 11:00 PM

Age Restriction: 18+

Share:

Event Description

Join us for the biggest summer music festival of the year! Experience an unforgettable night with top artists from around the world performing live at Central Park.

The Summer Music Festival brings together a diverse lineup of musicians spanning multiple genres, from pop and rock to electronic and hip-hop. This year's headliners include internationally acclaimed artists who will deliver electrifying performances on our state-of-the-art main stage.

In addition to the amazing music, the festival will feature:

  • Food vendors offering a variety of cuisines
  • Craft beer and specialty cocktail bars
  • Art installations and interactive experiences
  • Merchandise stands with exclusive festival gear

Don't miss this incredible celebration of music, art, and community in the heart of New York City!
